choose your corner

use top, bottom, tl, tr, bl, br to identify which corner to style

top bevel

$(this).corner("bevel top");

top-left bite

$(this).corner("bite tl");

round bottom

$(this).corner("bottom");

bottom-left notch

$(this).corner("notch bl");

top-right dog ear

$(this).corner("dog tr");

top-left, bottom-right cool
